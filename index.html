Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does your tool do?

Our smart system watches your paychecks and helps adjust how much tax is taken out of each one. By getting these numbers right, you can take home more money every month instead of waiting for a big tax refund once a year. Our AI continuously monitors your financial situation to detect changes in income, job status, or life events, then recommends precise withholding adjustments to keep your take-home pay optimized year-round.

Is this a replacement for tax filing software?

No. We focus specifically on optimizing your withholdings throughout the year, not filing your taxes. While tax filing software helps you once a year, we balance your W-4 withholdings to prevent both problems: too much withheld (where you're giving up $300+ per month in usable income) and too little withheld (which leads to surprise tax bills and potential penalties). Our goal is to get your withholdings just right - maximizing your monthly take-home pay without creating tax-time surprises.

How is this different from other financial tools? Is this like TurboTax?

No. TurboTax helps you file. Taxedo helps you fix what's wrong before you file — your W-4. And unlike budgeting apps, we directly increase your available income by ensuring you're not overpaying or underpaying taxes with each paycheck.

How do you keep my financial information secure?

We use bank-level encryption for all data, provide opt-in only connections to financial accounts, and offer automatic deletion options for sensitive documents after processing.

Is your platform accessible for users with disabilities?

Yes. We've built comprehensive accessibility features including screen-reader optimization, high-contrast visual modes, voice guidance, keyboard accessibility, and simplified language options.
